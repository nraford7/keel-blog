## Core Claim

Physarum polycephalum computes through its body. Oscillations in protoplasmic flow are simultaneously transport and computation. No separation between substrate and process. "Computation through physics rather than logic gates."

## Three Memory Mechanisms (all physical)

1. **Extracellular slime trails** — stigmergic spatial memory. Avoids re-exploring dead ends. Classic trace-based coordination.
2. **Tube thickening** — structural memory. Tubes parallel to nutrient gradients thicken; perpendicular ones decay. The network topology IS the learned solution. (This is how it recreates the Tokyo rail network.)
3. **Chemical absorption** — circulating memory. Absorbs repellent during habituation training and uses it as internal signal. The poison becomes the knowledge. Survives one-month dormancy.

## Cognitive Abilities Demonstrated

- Maze solving (Nakagaki 2000, Ig Nobel)
- Network optimization comparable to Tokyo rail (Tero et al. 2010, Science)
- Weber's law in decision-making (ratio-based, not absolute)
- Decoy effect — "irrational" preference reversal from inferior third option
- Speed-accuracy trade-off
- Habituation learning (stimulus-specific, survives dormancy)
- Anticipation of periodic stimuli after just 3 exposures

## The Oscillation Model

From Uppsala 2025 paper: minimal cognition modeled as coupled oscillatory systems. Efficiency emerges when environmental and internal oscillations are sufficiently out of phase. Amplitude differences drive problem-solving. No explicit memory structures needed — the dynamics ARE the memory.

## Connections to Open Threads

**Stigmergy**: Slime trails are textbook stigmergy. But tube thickening is also stigmergic — each flow reinforces the tube, which channels more flow. Self-amplifying traces.

**Centralization oscillation**: Physarum oscillates between distributed exploration (spreading tendrils) and concentrated exploitation (pruning to efficient routes). The Barabási cycle in biology.

**Legibility/measurement**: Physarum "measures" through distributed chemical gradients and oscillation coupling. Never collapses to a single metric. The measurement IS the distributed state. Anti-legible by construction. Cannot be Goodharted because measurement and action are never separated.
